Discursive Disability

A Poetic Analysis of 2022 U.S. Disability Statistics

  • Resting at my feet
  • The bottles circle like hawks
  • Empty and alone
  • Busy hands at work
  • Idle ones yearn for purpose
  • Potential stolen
  • The rich burn brightly
  • Searing, caustic, beating down
  • Dark ash in its wake
  • Books stacked like mountains
  • But the path winds, rough and steep
  • Pages out of reach
  • Years stack on frail bones
  • Each one heavier than last
  • Steps slow, shadows grow
  • The mind's tangled thread
  • Weaves knots no hand can untie
  • Still, they pull tighter
  • The threat of aging
  • Digging our premature graves,
  • Our only escape
  • Prosperity's road
  • is too steep a hill to climb,
  • with no ramp in sight
